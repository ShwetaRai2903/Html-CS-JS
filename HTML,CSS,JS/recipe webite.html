Chai Recipe

Ingrediants

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • Acoording to need add sugar
  • 2 sppons tea leaves
  • 2-3 fresh gingers
  • 2-3 cardamom (Elaichi)

Instructions

  1. Boil 2 cups of water
  2. then add ginger ,cardamom, sugar and tea leaves
  3. add milk
  4. and boil this
  5. And serve
